# Mini-story: adding "Mark as undone"

 Want to be able to un-do a top (NB: "Munro", "Top", and "top" with lower case means
 either of them). What does this look like?
  - Munros and Tops are coloured in as at present
  - the chart list below shows them as mostly greyed-out as at present
  - the "done" button remains active and has the text "re-open" on it
  - when you click it, the top is un-coloured and the chart list entry reverts to active

## Approach

 The challenge covers two use-cases:
  - Passive, when a chart is loaded and the top is flagged as "done" already
  - Active, when the user clicks the "Done" button and the playarea and info list change
